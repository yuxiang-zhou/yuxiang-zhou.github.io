Who am I?

I recieved my Ph.D in Computing at Imperial College London, UK, in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Stefanos Zafeiriou. Currently, I am a Research Engineer at DeepMind.

Research Topic: Solve Robotics. Third Person Imitation Learning. Research on Statistical Deformable Models with Dense Shapes. Estimate dense correspondence between shapes without landmark. Apply model fitting and tracking on highly deformable and articulated objects under unconstrained conditions.
